How to Play: Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Aces are worth 1 or 11.

üéØ Strategy: Basic strategy: hit on 16 or below, stand on 17 or above. Double down on 11, split 8s and Aces.

üí° Pro Tip: The dealer must hit on 16 and below, stand on 17 and above. Use this to your advantage!

How to Play: Bet on Player, Banker, or Tie. Two cards are dealt, and the hand closest to 9 wins.

üéØ Strategy: Banker bets have slightly better odds (1.06% house edge vs 1.24% for Player). Tie bets have high payouts but poor odds.

üí∞ Simple Rules: Face cards and 10s count as 0, Aces count as 1, other cards count as their face value.

üéØ Strategy: Pass Line and Don't Pass bets have the best odds. Avoid proposition bets with high house edges.

üí° Beginner Tip: Start with simple Pass Line bets until you understand the game better.

üéØ Strategy: Fewer numbers = better odds but lower payouts. More numbers = higher payouts but lower odds.

üí∞ Payouts: Payouts increase exponentially with more matches. A 10-number ticket with 10 matches pays the highest!
